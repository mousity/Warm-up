Scrambled Eggs

Description

Scrambled eggs! Take an egg, scramble it! Blend the egg, beat the egg, egg the egg. Scrambled egg!

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Crack eggs
  2. Beat eggs
  3. Melt butter on pan
  4. Cook eggs on pan
  5. Take eggs off pan and add salt
  6. Eggs acquired
